BWIS

Black Women in Science (BWIS) is an emerging researchers programme that aims to encourage women participation in STEM careers, technology, mentoring and education.

Our Stories

For every scientist whose work has been recognized and celebrated, there are thousands who have been accidentally or purposefully forgotten.... The contribution of women across all areas of science is still largely overlooked. Our BWIS fellows are front runners in numerous disciplines indeed. We celebrate the many exceptional women who did not allow societal barriers stop them from pursuing their scientific passions. Celebrate these exceptional women with us!

Central to the BWIS fellowship is the creation of ambassadors of excellence in breaking the mould for young ...women in science and business by mobilising youth and women leadership and igniting a passion for active citizenship and social cohesion.
